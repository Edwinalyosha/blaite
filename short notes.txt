established on the evening of 24th october 2014; the blaite revolution is a collective effort to remind humanity of the life we miss, the life we deserve, a life which could be if we dare to see each other as another necessary stroke of God's paintbrush. 

the blaite revolution is a family of artists, poets, dancers, actors,vocalists, you name it who have dedicated their craftsmanship towards the daunting and noble task of poking holes into the fabric of humanity's conflict. performing artists performing to outperform disunity.
